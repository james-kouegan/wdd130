Overview

Our goal is to meet the needs of our customers

Scenario Question:
1 Question: What course options are available for beginners?
Answer: For beginners, we offer easy to moderate rafting courses, with experienced guides to ensure safety and fun. Our routes include calm sections and rapids suitable for novices.
2 Question: What equipment is needed for a rafting trip?
Answer: Essential equipment includes a life jacket, helmet, paddle and inflatable boat. We provide all necessary equipment, but participants must bring water shoes, swimwear and sunscreen.
3 Question: What are the options for families with young children?
Answer: We have family courses suitable for children, with calmer waters and magnificent landscapes. Families can enjoy a safe rafting experience, with guides trained to look after the youngest.
